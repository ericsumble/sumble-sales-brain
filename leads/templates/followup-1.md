# Follow-up #1 (+4 days)

Sent 4 days after the intro if no reply. Threaded into the same Gmail thread via `replyToMessageId` so it lands as a reply, not a new conversation.

Rules unchanged from intro: plain text, under 100 words, no sentence over 20 words, no bullets, no em-dashes. Subject is inherited from the thread; do not set a new one.

Per Sales Brain re-engagement rule: **fresh angle, different play or different signal**. Do not repeat the intro's pitch. Do not open with "Welcome aboard."

## Body shape

```
Hi {first_name},

Wanted to circle back since {intro_date}.

One more angle. {Different non-obvious signal mapped to a different play for the same buyer.} {Concrete claim about what that signal predicts.} ZoomInfo cannot tag that. Sumble can.

{Low-friction reply ask, even softer than intro. "Worth 10 minutes?" works.}

Eric
Account Executive | Sumble
https://calendly.com/eric-sumble/30min
```

## Selection logic for the "different angle"

Pick from a play they did not see in the intro. If the intro led with hiring signal, the follow-up leads with tech-stack displacement or change-event. If the intro led with one industry vertical, this leads with a second.
